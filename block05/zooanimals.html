  1. Bears

    1. Ollie
    2. Mona

    Bears are mammals. They are big, strong and ferocious. The mother bears are very protective of their young. You should avoid surprising a bear by making lots of noise while hiking.

  2. Giraffes

    1. Frankie
    2. Coconut

    Giraffes have long necks, but surprisingly, they have the same number of neck bones like us. With their long necks, they are able to reach leaves of tall trees.

  5. Alligators

    1. Wren
    2. Aspen
    3. Mika

    Aligators have A-shaped heads which is much wider than the narrow-shaped heads of crocodiles. Their jaws are strong and are virtually imposssible to open once clamped around their victims. Although they are often found seemingly asleep on the riverbanks, they are actually awake waiting for unknowing creatures to move close in order to quickly capture them with their jaws.
